Welcome to OCSA Hack Club!

We are a club for High Schoolers interested in coding and hacking. We meet every Friday at lunch(sometimes Office Hours) in DMS 221(Mr. Chong's Room). We welcome people of all experiences! If you have any questions, please email us at team@ocsa.hackclub.com

In semester 1 we'll be learning coding basics, like python, and also be making webistes! In semester 2 we'll do some YSWS projects such as Blot, Sprig, or hackaccino
